Come and experience the difference!

Yummi Cafe Laundromat is your friendly neighbourhood cafe and laundry services, servicing Regal Heights, Wynchwood, Oakwood, Corso Italia, Hillcrest Village and St. Clair Ave. West.

Services we offer

  • Coin Laundry
  • Wash and Fold Service (Drop off and Delivery)

Products we offer

  • Nellie's Laundry Soda (refills availabe)
  • Nylon Laundry Bags (with shoulder straps)
  • Cafe Sandwiches, Expresso Drinks and Kombucha
  • Kawartha Dairy Ice Cream
  • Take-home Pints and Kits

Family friendly atmosphere

Our laundromat cafe is clean, bright and airy, with a kids books section to keep the little ones busy while you do your laundry or have a coffee. With a full service cafe, if you get hungry, you can try some of our Yummi sandwiches, soups, pastries, kawartha's ice cream and much much more!

Environmentally friendly

We are committed to keeping our footprint as small as possible with;

  • All new energy and water efficient machines.
  • All lighting in our store are LEDs.
  • All our TVs are also energy efficient.
  • Diverting tons of used clothing from landfill and to charities.
  • Offering natural and eco-friendly detergents.
